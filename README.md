# Freelancer-Excel-Dashboard
The dataset contains detailed information about freelancers working on a digital platform. Each row represents one freelancer, and the columns provide key attributes related to their activity, performance, and background.
Project Objectives:
	To analyse the distribution of Active vs Inactive freelancers
	To compare hourly rates across genders
	To study the relationship between years of experience and number of projects
	To evaluate the correlation between hourly rate and project count
	To develop an interactive dashboard 
FEATURES:
	Freelancer_ID – Unique identification code assigned to each freelancer
	Is Active – Indicates whether the freelancer is currently active (YES) or inactive (NO) on the platform.
	Name – The full name of the freelancer.
	Gender – Specifies whether the freelancer is Male or Female.
	Age – Age of the freelancer
	Age – Age of the freelancer
	Hourly Rate ($) – The amount the freelancer charges per hour for their services.
	Years of Experience – Total number of years the freelancer has worked in their field.
	No. of Projects – The number of projects the freelancer has completed so far.
	Project Status – Quality level of projects (High / Medium / Low).
	Duration_months – Time taken to complete recent projects.
INSIGHTS:
DESCRIPTIVE ANALYSIS
Descriptive analysis summarises the basic characteristics of the dataset. It helps you understand the overall picture of the freelancers.
DIAGNOSTIC ANALYSIS
Diagnostic analysis helps us understand the reasons behind the patterns observed in the descriptive stage.
CONCLUSION:
The dataset shows that most freelancers are active and experienced, which leads to higher project counts and better performance. Ratings and experience strongly influence hourly rates and project quality. There is no major gender difference in earnings, indicating fair opportunities. Overall, the dataset gives a clear picture of freelancer productivity, engagement, and earning trends
